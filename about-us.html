From a single salon in Thrissur to one of Kerala's most trusted premium beauty chains — built on expertise, genuine care, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

"Hair Studio De Luxe is more than a salon. It is a sanctuary where artistry meets relaxation, and every detail is tailored to elevate your natural beauty — quality should never be compromised."

— The Hair Studio De Luxe Philosophy

Our Story

Where It All Began

ATMOSOTWELL CARE BEAUTY SERVICES LLP, now operating as Hair Studio De Luxe, began its journey in 2025 with a simple dream: to deliver world-class quality services with genuine care. The first outlet opened its doors in Fathima Nagar, Thrissur — the renowned 'Cultural and Gold Capital of Kerala.'

Our heritage dates back to 2018 when our first store opened in Thrissur with a small but passionate contingent of 6 professionals who were eager to bring a new level of excellence to hair and beauty services in the region.

Today, ATMOSOTWELL CARE BEAUTY SERVICES LLP operates two distinctive brands — L Studio and Hair Studio De Luxe — through a robust base of over 100 expert professionals. Every outlet promises the luxurious experience our clients await at each of our branches.
